GET YOUR M1 LICENSE FAST!

Our DMV waiver makes the process simple. Upon successful completion of the MTC or 1DPC, you can skip the riding test at the DMV.

  • 1Pass the written exam at the DMV.
  • 2Successfully complete our MTC or 1DPC course.
  • 3Return to the DMV with our completion certificate to get your license!
